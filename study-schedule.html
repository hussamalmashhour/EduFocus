Why You Need a Study Schedule

A well-structured study schedule helps you manage time, avoid cramming, and reduce stress. With consistency and planning, your academic performance improves significantly.

How to Create Your Study Plan

  1. Define your academic goals
  2. List your subjects and deadlines
  3. Allocate time blocks for each topic
  4. Use digital or printable templates
  5. Review and adjust your schedule weekly

Useful Tools & Apps

  • Todoist – Task Planner
  • Trello – Visual Task Management
  • Google Calendar
