Why Mental Health Matters

Studying under pressure can lead to stress, burnout, and anxiety. Taking care of your mental well-being is just as important as maintaining good grades.

Focus & Relaxation Tools

  • Insight Timer – Free meditation app
  • Calm – Guided mindfulness and sleep aid
  • Yana – Mental health support via journaling
  • Headspace – Meditation and stress relief

Healthy Study Habits

  • Take short breaks every 25–50 minutes
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Use soothing music or ambient sounds while studying
