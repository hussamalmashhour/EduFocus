What is Time Blocking?

Time blocking is a productivity method where your day is divided into blocks of time, and each block is dedicated to a specific task. It helps eliminate distractions and boosts deep focus.

How to Start Time Blocking

  1. List out your daily tasks and priorities
  2. Allocate fixed time slots to each task on a calendar
  3. Stick to the schedule and minimize multitasking
  4. Review and adjust your blocks weekly
